# Integrated Product Flow & S&OP Portfolio

This portfolio demonstrates how I approach end-to-end product flow, Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP), inventory strategy, operational risk management, and cross-functional execution across supply chain environments.

It is designed to showcase my ability to connect planning, inventory flow, distribution execution, analytics, and executive communication to support on-time product availability and strong customer outcomes.

The portfolio reflects a practical leadership approach built from experience in distribution center operations, supply chain process improvement, analytics, and business-focused decision support.

## Portfolio Sections

- **01_SOP_Framework** – Sales & Operations Planning structure, cadence, and planning discipline
- **02_Product_Flow_Model** – End-to-end product flow from supplier to distribution center to store/customer
- **03_KPI_Dashboard** – Operational and strategic supply chain KPIs
- **04_Risk_Management** – Fact-based risk identification, escalation, and mitigation planning
- **05_Playbooks_and_SOPs** – Standardized operating playbooks and process documentation
- **06_Executive_Communication** – Executive summaries, structured updates, and decision materials
- **07_Tools_Python_Tableau_Excel** – Analytics tools and technical support assets
- **08_90_Day_Strategy** – Sample strategic plan for assessing and improving product flow

## Purpose

This repository is intended to demonstrate my ability to simplify complex supply chain concepts, align cross-functional stakeholders, and use data to improve product flow performance.
