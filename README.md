# Integrated Product Flow & S&OP Portfolio

## Overview

This portfolio demonstrates how I design and execute end-to-end product flow strategies by aligning Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP), inventory management, distribution operations, and cross-functional execution.

My approach focuses on ensuring the right product is in the right place at the right time by combining structured planning, operational discipline, data-driven decision making, and clear executive communication.

This work reflects practical application built from experience in distribution center operations, supply chain analytics, and process improvement, with a strong emphasis on simplifying complex concepts to drive alignment and results.

---

## Core Capabilities Demonstrated

- **End-to-End Product Flow Strategy**
  - Supplier → Distribution Center → Store → Customer alignment
- **Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP)**
  - Demand and supply balancing, planning cadence, and execution tracking
- **Inventory Flow Optimization**
  - Positioning, replenishment logic, and stockout prevention
- **Operational Risk Management**
  - Identification, escalation, and mitigation of product flow risks
- **Cross-Functional Leadership**
  - Alignment across Merchandising, Supply Chain, Inventory, and Store Operations
- **Data & Analytics for Decision Support**
  - KPI development, dashboards, and performance monitoring
- **Executive Communication**
  - Translating operational insights into clear, actionable business decisions

---

## Portfolio Structure

### 01_SOP_Framework
Defines the structure, cadence, and governance of Sales & Operations Planning, including demand planning, supply alignment, and execution tracking.

### 02_Product_Flow_Model
Illustrates the end-to-end movement of product from supplier to customer, including flow design, constraints, and operational dependencies.

### 03_KPI_Dashboard
Provides visibility into key supply chain performance metrics such as on-time delivery, fill rate, inventory turns, and stockout rates.

### 04_Risk_Management
Outlines a structured approach to identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks that impact product flow and customer outcomes.

### 05_Playbooks_and_SOPs
Includes standardized playbooks to support consistent execution across planning, operations, and peak event readiness.

### 06_Executive_Communication
Demonstrates how operational insights are translated into executive-level updates, recommendations, and decision frameworks.

### 07_Tools_Python_Tableau_Excel
Highlights the tools and analytical methods used to support visibility, reporting, and continuous improvement.

### 08_90_Day_Strategy
Presents a structured approach to assessing, stabilizing, and optimizing product flow within the first 90 days of leadership.

---

## Business Impact Focus

The strategies and frameworks in this portfolio are designed to:

- Improve on-time product delivery to stores and customers  
- Reduce stockouts and excess inventory  
- Increase visibility across the end-to-end supply chain  
- Enable faster, data-driven decision making  
- Align cross-functional teams around shared operational goals

Supply Chain & Distribution Operations Leader with a focus on inventory optimization, product flow execution, and data-driven decision making. Experienced in connecting operational performance with strategic business outcomes through analytics, process improvement, and cross-functional leadership.
